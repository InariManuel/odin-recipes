Patacones, bitch

Description

Fried pain-banana

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel Plantain, and cut it width wise into 3 or 4 pieces.
  2. Heat 1" of vegetable oil on medium heat until hot.
  3. Fry plantain pieces on both sides for about 3 minutes, or until the pieces are golden.
  4. Flatten the fried plantain until they are about 1/4in thick.
  5. Place in the hot oil again and fry until both sides are golden brown.
  6. Flatten plantains again until they are 1/8in thick
  7. Fry plantains again.
  8. Flatten plantains until they are see-through.
  9. Fry again.
  10. Fry plantain oil.
  11. Open your fridge and see what else can be fried.
  12. Fry your condiments.
  13. Fry your roommates homework.
  14. Fry insects from under your picnic table in the backyard.
  15. Invite your neighbors to come over and fry things.
  16. Run for political office on the platform "Everything can be fried."
  17. Have a heart-attack.
  18. Sneak into hospital cafeteria and fry the little chocolate puddings they gave you.
  19. Place plantains on paper towels.
  20. Serve immediately.
