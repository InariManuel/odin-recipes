Patacones

Description

Patacones are cut-up pieces of plantain fried in oil until golden brown, then drained and flattened. After flattening, the plantains are deep-fried again. This specialty dish is popular in the Caribbean, Latin America, and other countries where plantains are abundant.

Ingredients

  • Green plantains
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel Plantain, and cut it width wise into 3 or 4 pieces.
  2. Heat 1" of vegetable oil on medium heat until hot.
  3. Fry plantain pieces on both sides for about 3 minutes, or until the pieces are golden.
  4. Flatten the fried plantain until they are about 1/4in thick.
  5. Place in the hot oil again and fry until both sides are golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towel covered plate and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Serve Immediately
