Homemade Corn Tortillas

Description

Tortillas are unleavened flat breads made with corn or wheat flour, water, and salt.

Ingredients

  • Masa harina
  • Hot water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, briefly whisk together masa harina and salt.
  2. Gradually add 1 1/2 cups hot water, and stir the mixture with a wooden spoon or silicone spatula until an evenly-mixed dough begins to form.
  3. Use your hands to knead the dough for 2-3 minutes in the mixing bowl until it is smooth and forms a cohesive ball.
  4. Cover the bowl with a damp kitchen towel (or paper towel) and let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Use a spoon or a medium ice cream scoop to portion the dough into a 2-tablespoon ball then use your hands to roll the ball until it is nice and round.
  6. Place the dough ball between two pieces of plastic in a tortilla press. Then gently press the dough ball until it forms a 4- to 5-inch tortilla.
  7. Cook the tortilla for about 40-60 seconds per side, flipping it once speckled brown spots begin to appear on the bottom of the tortilla.
